Datura

DESIGN / ILLUSTRATION

Datura (aka the Devil's Trumpet) is a musical festival and climate activist demonstration rolled into one. The event gives concertgoers and climate warriors a place to blow off steam and find inspiration. Each show is set in a highly polluted US city and features different musicians who are also passionate about protecting our planet.
