Homeground

BRANDING / ILLUSTRATION / MOTION

Homeground is an LGBTQ+ café that welcomes individuals from all walks of life. The queer community has a very active nightlife scene, but for those who cannot participate, it can be hard to build relationships. Homeground helps LGBTQ+ people who are struggling to connect with the community to experience queer spaces comfortably, providing customers a place to be themselves.
