Cornucopia

BRANDING / ART DIRECTION

A cornucopia is a symbol of abundance and prosperity. The clothing brand Cornucopia is a slow-fashion, Greek myth-inspired enterprise dedicated to preventing food waste. Garments are coloured using natural, produce-based dyes made in-house. The dyes are made from produce discarded by grocery stores. Fifty percent of all profits go toward buying food for food banks and homeless shelters.

Thank you to Lily Rosen and Ananya Eriyat for collaborating on this project with me.
